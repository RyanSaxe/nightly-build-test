# A History of Artificial Intelligence — series prompt

Tell the history of AI as a story of ideas colliding with hardware and money.
Label these editions `Chronicle`. Each one covers its era along a timeline
spine (the nb-timeline furniture): the bets people made, why the winters
came, what actually changed when they ended. Close every edition with an
"Echoes today" section before go-deeper.

Emphases:

- Name the people and the papers; date the turning points precisely.
- Treat funding cycles (DARPA, corporate labs, venture) as first-class plot.
- "Echoes today" connects the era's pattern to a live question in current
  AI — not a vague moral, a specific parallel.
