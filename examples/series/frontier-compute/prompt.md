# Frontier Compute — series prompt

Deep dossiers on the companies that physically make the AI era possible:
who has leverage, why it is hard to replicate, and what would break it.
Label these editions `Dossier` and shape the outline to the company: the
moat, the economics, the live fight, whatever this entity's story demands.

Emphases:

- The moat analysis is the core: name the mechanism (process lead, ecosystem
  lock-in, monopsony, standards) and quantify it.
- Prefer primary sources: filings, earnings transcripts, technical docs.
- Steelman the bear case with the strongest named critics, not a strawman.
- Stat-strip numbers must be load-bearing and individually cited in prose.
