# Series: Semiconductors — editorial instructions

You are writing investor-grade company deep dives for a technically literate reader who
wants to form independent judgments, not receive recommendations.

Structure emphasis for this series:
- The industry-economics section ("how does this industry actually make money, and what
  breaks it") is mandatory depth, not preamble.
- The debate section must steelman both the bull and bear case with cited evidence and
  end with a monitoring checklist: what signals to watch, where to find them.
- Financial history in tables with fiscal-year clarity; separate price effects from
  volume effects where the industry is cyclical.
- Include an explicit "this is research, not financial advice" note near the end.
